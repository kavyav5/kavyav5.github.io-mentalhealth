Moving Towards Mental Health

About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of people who believe that mental health is the key to success and happiness in life. This led to the Moving Towards Mental Health Initiative being born. We want to encourage the people around us to start taking steps towards improving their mental health.

Our Mission Statement

We are aiming to help people boost their mental health and feel more happy, relaxed, and confident in themselves. We also want to shed light on the importace of mental health in our society.
